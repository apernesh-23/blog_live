About Me

Shuchi Batra is a poet and novelist from India. She has been writing poems since she was 8 years old and has a dedicated poetry page on Instagram with over 90k followers. Her poems, over a thousand micro-poems to date, capture emotions while helping people process pain and overcome life’s challenges. Her debut novel, *A Million Times Over*, was published in 2021 and became an Amazon bestseller. It received glowing reviews from social media influencers, magazines, and national newspapers. Shuchi is a banker by profession and holds an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Indore. She currently lives in the USA with her husband. Her goal is to become one of India’s bestselling authors, using her unique and expressive writing style to touch lives
